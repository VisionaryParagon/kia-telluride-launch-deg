ADAS Touchpoint Tour

Telluride introduces the mid-size SUV segment’s most comprehensive standard suite of advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS). Take a look around the Telluride and see if you can identify the various external sensing systems that power a wide range of ADAS features.

Exterior ADAS Systems Overview

While ADAS is integrated throughout the vehicle, the exterior ADAS sensors play a vital part in detecting objects in real-time. Can you spot each of these sensors?

Radar (see front/rear bumpers): Radar uses radio waves to measure the presence and distance of objects. LiDAR (Light Detection And Ranging radar—see front windshield) uses a stream of lasers to measure distance, interpret road signs, markings, and support Level 2 semi-autonomous driving. Cameras (front grille, rear tailgate, OSRV mirrors): When activated, four cameras offer live video feed of driving conditions. During reverse or low-speed driving, the four cameras are “stitched together” to provide a surround view of the Telluride’s perimeter. ADAS External Tour and Discussion
